City of Jehovah aids other religions

City of Jehovah, a local NGO, recently provided uniforms and food supplies to help underprivileged school boys continue their education.

The City of Jehovah, a well-respected non-governmental organization (NGO), is making a positive impact on the lives of underprivileged children in Lusaka, Zambia.

Led by the esteemed CEO, Mr. Cephas Zulu, the organization strives to provide essential resources to children facing hardship.

City of Jehovah focuses on addressing two critical needs of underprivileged children: access to proper uniforms and nutritious food.

The City of Jehovah's ongoing efforts are changing the lives of underprivileged children in Lusaka. By providing essential resources like uniforms and food, the organization empowers children to reach their full potential.
